My Story

The Past

A quick background: I graduated from the computer science program at Camosun College in 2012. For the last 6 years I worked as an implementation consultant for Fast Enterprises. Fast provides large ERP tax solutions to governments. It was a great job, but once the size of the company expanded, the ability for developers to modify and customize the system diminished, and developers really started to take on more consulting roles than development. The development here was mainly based on MS SQL Server and VB.NET, which personally, I'm not too keen on.
The golden handcuffs. Problem is I enjoy being a developer. I had to make a choice, so I decided take a chance, to leave so I could build up my skills in the technologies I am passionate about.

 
 

The Future

I've always enjoyed Javascript so I made this my foundation, brushed up my skills in the basics, HTML, CSS, and plain old Javascript. After that I worked on building up my skills in Node, React, React Native, and NextJS.
However, the technology that really gets me excited is Blockchain. I first started researching Blockchain a few years ago, and eventually I realized there was a market for spreading excitement about cryptocurrencies and the Blockchain, so I wrote and published a book on the subject to explain the technology for people wanting to get into the field or just wanting to understand what its all about (available at the Amazon Book Store).

Now I am learning as much as I possibly can about the Blockchain, Ethereum, Solidity, and Smart Contracts. Which is also the reason why I have chosen React and NextJS in the first place, as they are great technologies to interact with the Ethereum network via web applications.
Leaving my old job behind was the best decision I could have made. I have thoroughly enjoy learning new technologies and building the projects that I've been wanting to build.
My ultimate goal is to find a position where I can keep applying my skills and keep learning as much as possible.
And even though I live on beautiful Vancouver Island in BC, I am willing to move wherever I have to to realize this goal.
